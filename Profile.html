About Me

Hello! I'm Prem Mandal, an Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate from NIST University, Berhampur. I'm passionate about technology and its applications in solving real-world problems.

My educational journey began at Sri Satya Sai Vidya Bihar, Bhanjanagar, where I completed my 10th standard. I then pursued my 12th grade at Nalanda Vidya Mandir, Berhampur, where I developed a strong foundation in science and mathematics.

During my B.Tech at NIST University, I gained comprehensive knowledge in electrical systems, electronics, and programming. The curriculum provided me with hands-on experience in various technical domains, sparking my interest in software development alongside my core electrical engineering studies.

I have developed skills in programming languages like C, MATLAB, and web technologies including HTML and CSS. I'm continuously learning and expanding my knowledge in Node.js and other emerging technologies to enhance my capabilities as a developer.

I'm eager to apply my technical skills and problem-solving abilities to contribute to innovative projects and grow professionally in the tech industry.
